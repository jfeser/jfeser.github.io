Currently, I am a PhD student at MIT CSAIL, working with Armando Solar Lezama. I received my Undergraduate and Master's degrees from Rice University, where I worked with Swarat Chaudhuri. I spent the summer of 2015 working at Microsoft Research (Redmond) with Sumit Gulwani and Rishabh Singh, and the summer of 2016 working at Microsoft Research (Cambridge) with Marc Brockschmidt and Daniel Tarlow.

My research interests are in automated program synthesis and program analysis.
